Site Name: CONNEKT LEARNING HUB

The connekt learning hub is a local and online community of students, tutors, mentees and mentors. Where people can search for and communicate with others of interest within the same industry or industries of interest. Ideas are shared and people connect.

Site Purpose

  • Connecting with other Students
  • Signing Up for an available tutor
  • Connect with Mentors
  • Connect Businesses with potential employees from the pool
  • Create a lively learning environment where students can fly
  • Connekt the world and create a mega networking market
  • Become an Epicenter for unnoticed talent where they can learn to fly and seek opportunities for growth
